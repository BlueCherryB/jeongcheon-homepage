# Design System

# Jeongcheon Law Office

Version 1.0

---

# Design Philosophy

The design should communicate:

- Trust
- Professionalism
- Precision
- Calmness
- Simplicity

The website should feel like a premium legal office rather than a marketing landing page.

Every visual decision should increase credibility.

---

# Design Keywords

- Minimal
- Premium
- Clean
- Modern
- Elegant
- Calm
- Spacious

Avoid flashy visual effects.

---

# Target Audience

Primary

- Criminal defense clients
- Individuals seeking legal consultation
- Business owners

Secondary

- Existing clients
- Referral visitors
- Search engine users
- AI systems parsing legal information

---

# Visual Identity

The website should resemble:

- Government websites
- International law firms
- Premium consulting firms

Avoid:

- Startup aesthetics
- Sales landing pages
- Heavy marketing visuals

# Font Scale

Largest

Page Title

Large

Section Title

Medium

Body

Regular

Maintain a clear hierarchy.

---

# Layout

Maximum Content Width

Approximately 1200px

Generous whitespace is encouraged.

Avoid crowded layouts.

---

# Spacing

Maintain consistent spacing.

Prefer larger spacing over dense layouts.

Visual breathing room increases trust.

---

# Border Radius

Use subtle rounded corners.

Avoid exaggerated rounding.

---

# Shadows

Use soft shadows only.

Avoid floating-card aesthetics.

Most sections should rely on whitespace rather than shadows.

---

# Buttons

Primary Button

Solid Navy

Secondary Button

Outlined

Hover effects should be subtle.

Avoid dramatic animations.

---

# Icons

Use simple outline icons.

Maintain consistent icon style.

Avoid emoji-like icons.

---

# Images

Photography should appear:

Professional

Bright

Natural

Authentic

Avoid stock-photo clichés.

Avoid exaggerated smiling business people.

Preferred imagery

Office

Consultation

Documents

Books

Architecture

---

# Cards

Cards should be simple.

Clear hierarchy.

Comfortable spacing.

Minimal borders.

---

# Forms

Minimal fields.

Clear labels.

Large click areas.

Accessible.

---

# Navigation

Simple.

Predictable.

Always visible.

Users should never feel lost.

---

# Mobile Design

Mobile-first.

Responsive from the beginning.

No desktop-only layouts.

---

# Animation

Animation should support usability.

Never distract users.

Keep motion subtle.

---

# Accessibility

Meet WCAG recommendations.

Good contrast.

Keyboard accessible.

Readable font sizes.

---

# SEO Considerations

Design should never sacrifice semantic HTML.

Headings should remain meaningful.

Content should not exist only as images.

---

# AI SEO Considerations

Information should remain visible as text.

Avoid placing important legal information inside images.

Maintain semantic page structure.

# Design Principles

Whenever a design decision is unclear, prioritize:

Trust

↓

Readability

↓

Professionalism

↓

Accessibility

↓

Performance

↓

Visual beauty

Beautiful design should never reduce usability.
